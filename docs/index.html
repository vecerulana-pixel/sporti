Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sporti Android application handles information when you follow sports events, read news, save favorites, write notes, use the coach stopwatch, and view personal analytics.

Effective date: July 23, 2026

No account requiredSporti does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or login.

1. Scope and developer

This policy applies to Sporti (Android package com.sporti.app), developed and published by vecerulana-pixel ("we," "us," or "our"). It does not govern websites or services operated by third parties.

2. Information handled by Sporti

Sporti does not create user accounts and does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, payment details, advertising identifier, photos, microphone data, or health data.

The following content is created or stored locally on your device:

  • notes that you write in the app;
  • matches and news items that you mark as favorites;
  • stopwatch durations, lap times, and the time a training session was saved;
  • personal statistics calculated from your locally stored activity; and
  • cached match schedules and news items downloaded for offline access.

This locally stored content is not uploaded to us and is not used to identify you.

3. Network access and third-party services

Sporti uses an internet connection to download sports schedules from TheSportsDB and sports headlines from BBC Sport. When you open a news article, it is opened in your web browser and the destination website's own terms and privacy policy apply.

As with any internet request, these service operators and network infrastructure may automatically receive limited technical information such as your IP address, request date and time, and a standard network user-agent value. We do not receive, combine, or retain that technical information.

For more information, review the TheSportsDB Privacy Policy and the BBC Privacy and Cookies Policy.

This policy page is hosted by GitHub Pages. When you visit it, GitHub may process standard connection and usage information under the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

4. How information is used and shared

Local information is used only to provide the features you request: saving notes and favorites, recording stopwatch sessions, calculating personal analytics, and making downloaded content available offline.

We do not sell, rent, monetize, or share your locally stored content. Sporti does not display personalized advertising and does not use cross-app tracking.

5. Device permissions

Sporti requests only the Android internet permission, which is required to retrieve match and news content. It does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, call logs, messages, or shared device storage.

6. Security

Sporti stores local information in the app's private Android storage, which is isolated by the operating system. Supported network requests use HTTPS encryption in transit. No security method is perfect, but we limit data handling to what is necessary for the app's functions.

7. Retention and deletion

Notes, favorites, cached content, and stopwatch sessions remain on your device until you delete them through the available app controls, clear Sporti's storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app. Clearing app storage or uninstalling Sporti permanently removes all app-managed local information from that device. Because we do not operate user accounts or receive this local information, there is no server-side account or personal database for us to delete.

8. Children's privacy

Sporti is a general-audience sports utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a linked third-party service, please contact that service and notify us using the contact method below.

9. International processing

The third-party sports and news services used by Sporti may operate servers in different countries. Their handling of network information is governed by their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when Sporti's features, data practices, or legal requirements change. The revised policy will be published at this same URL with an updated effective date. Material changes will be reflected in the app or its store listing when appropriate.

11. Contact and privacy requests

For questions, concerns, or privacy requests, contact the developer through the Sporti privacy inquiry form on GitHub. Please do not include sensitive personal information in a public issue.
